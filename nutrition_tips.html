what if there are no fresh vegtables/ fruits avaliable?

Alternative options

Alternative options can be checking out canned fruits options or canned vegtables option.

one main key takeaway is avoid vegtables with added salts and sugar.

* To reduce sodium consumption when using canned vegetables, it is important to rinse them thoroughly to wash away excess salt [1].

*When selecting canned goods, it is always helpful to avoid dented cans because damage can compromise the seal, potentially leading to bacterial contamination.

* When selecting canned fruits to avoid excess sugar, look for those packed "in water" instead of "in 100% juice". Especially the ones in syrup.
