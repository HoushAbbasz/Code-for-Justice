Get involved

Learn. Donate. Take Action Against Malnutrition.

This page provides a collection of trusted resources,organizations, and initiatives dedicated to fighting malnutrition around the world. Here, you can explore websites where you can learn more about the causes and solutions to malnutrition, donate to global efforts that provide nutritious food and support to vulnerable communities, and find local food pantries that offer assistance if you or someone you know is in need. Together, we can make a difference by supporting access to healthy food and promoting nutrition for all.

Feed The Children

Feed The Children is a nonprofit organization dedicated to ending childhood hunger and providing hope for families in need. They work to deliver food, education, and essential resources to children and their communities. They partner with local schools, community groups, and international agencies to ensure children receive nutritious meals, access to clean water, and the support needed to break the cycle of poverty and malnutrition.

Hope Street Food Pantry

Hope Street Food Pantry is located in Charlotte, North Carolina, is a barrier-free food pantry committed to ensuring that no one is he community goes hungry. Founded in 2018 in response to a growing need for accessible food support, the pantry now provides fresh produce, meat, pantry staples, and more.

  • Address: 4100 Johnston Oehler Road, Charlotte, NC 28269
  • Distrubution Hours: Thursdays from 4:00pm to 7:00pm at the main site.

Save The Children

Save the Children is a global humanitarian organization dedicated to improving the lives of children through better education, health care, and economic opportunities, as well as providing emergency aid in natural disasters, war, and other conflicts.

Defeat Malnutrition Today

Defeat Malnutrition Today is a national coalition working to advance policies and programs that prevent and treat malnutrition across the lifespan. Led by a diverse alliance of advocacy organizations, healthcare professionals, and community groups, the coalition raises awareness about the importance of nutrition in maintaining health and reducing healthcare costs.
